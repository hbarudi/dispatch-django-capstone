About Dispatch Django

Dispatch Django is a premier news platform designed to empower independent journalists while providing readers with high-quality, curated content.

Our platform facilitates a transparent relationship between reporters, editors, and subscribers. Whether you are reporting on local events or global trends, Dispatch provides the tools you need to reach your audience.

Platform Capabilities:

  • Journalists: Publish independently or via affiliated news organizations.
  • Editors: Ensure journalistic standards through a robust approval queue.
  • Readers: Subscribe to specific journalists to receive exclusive newsletters.
  • API: Access news data via our secure RESTful interface.
